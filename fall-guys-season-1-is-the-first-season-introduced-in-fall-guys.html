    Fall Guys Review

    If you are interested in colorful and fun games, Fall Guys is definitely a perfect choice. This game is one of the most popular battle royale games in recent years. With exciting gameplay, adorable and colorful design, and unique costumes, Fall Guys has attracted several players. Moreover, this game usually changed its theme and add new things to magnetize new player and retain the existing players.

    Season 1

    Season 1 is the first season introduced in Fall Guys with the nickname of Ultimate Knockout. It started on 4 August 2020 and ended on 8 October 2020 with the launch and change of rounds and obstacles. There is no unique theme for this season, however, it is the basic design of Fall Guys to develop the following seasons.

    Season 2

    Season 2 of Fall Guys was released directly after the end of Season 1 and ended on 15 December 2020. With the nickname of Medieval Knockout, this Season also had Medieval theme as it was named. There are many rounds and obstacles under this seasonâ€™s theme such as Wall Guys, Knight Fever rounds, and Swinging Axes obstacles were supplemented in Fall Guys.

    Season 3

    After the success of Season 2, Season 3 of Fall Guys launched with the nickname of Winter Knockout and Winter theme. The game was re-decorated with snow, ice, and several winter things as well as winter costumes such as penguin or snowball. 7 new rounds and many obstacles related to winter were also added in Season 3.
