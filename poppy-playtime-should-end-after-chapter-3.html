    Poppy Review

    It may be best if the Poppy Playtime series ends after Chapter 3, instead of dragging itself out and risking becoming stale and repetitive.

    When Chapter 3 of Poppy Playtime comes out

    When Chapter 3 of Poppy Playtime comes out, it could be best if it's the end of the series. It's unknown when exactly Chapter 3 will release or what it will entail, but from hints in Ch. 2, it would appear that answers are coming for many of the games' mysteries, such as how and why people were transformed into monstrous toys within the Playtime Co. factory. The developers may want to move on to other projects and leave Poppy Playtime behind, instead of continuing the series into the foreseeable future.

    As they explore the abandoned toy factory

    As they explore the abandoned toy factory, players' ultimate goal is to discover what happened to Poppy Playtime's Playtime Co. employees. Over time, it becomes evident that they were used for twisted human experiments that turned them into monstrous toys, the process for which was partially revealed in Chapter 2. It's also revealed that children were used for the experiments, orphans among them, which makes them even worse. The end of Ch. 2 teased an area of the factory known as Playcare as the setting of Ch. 3, and it may be where players will finally see exactly what happened to the employees, and how the toys were created first-hand.

    Poppy Playtime Chapter 3 Should End The Series

    Theoretically, Poppy Playtime could go on for quite some time. Its popularity has only grown, and if a Five Nights at Freddy's-style Poppy Playtime fanverse initiative were to ever happen, fan games could keep the series alive long after MOB Games ceases work on it. However, few game series can last forever, and although Poppy Playtime is fun and intriguing, it may not enjoy the longevity that household names like PokÃ©mon do. Ending after Chapter 3 could be best for it for a few different reasons.
