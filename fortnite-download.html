Fortnite: A legendary battle royale game
Fortnite at first just was a simple third-person shooter game, however, Epic Games has developed to make it become a global phenomenon. This game launched in 2017 with two different game modes released as early access including Save the World and Battle Royale. After five years released, Fortnite still stands out as one of the most popular games in the world. For both existing players and newbies, Fortnite always takes the role of an amazing battle royale game.
Fortnite Entering the Fortnite world
Entering the Fortnite world, players not only join in the shooting battle but also experience other activities such as building structures, protecting survivors, etc. There are three different game modes in Fortnite including Battle Royale, Save the World, and Creative. Battle Royale is player versus player game for up to 100 people which allows players to play alone, in a duo, or a squad. This game mode will bring players to the Island and fight with others to be the last one surviving.
